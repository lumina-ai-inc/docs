Chunkr turns complex PDFs, spreadsheets, and images into clean, LLM‑ready data - fast, accurate, and at scale.
We build industry leading VLMs + traditional computer-vision to deliver structured, machine-readable outputs.

This guide contains everything you need to understand Chunkr. If anything is missing,
we're here to help at support@chunkr.ai.
